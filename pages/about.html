About this site

Editorial voice: we on these pages; a little “I” here for the human behind the byline.

I’m Vivek Sinha — an engineer by heart who’s logged 1,000+ titles and on the order of 8,500+ watch hours on IMDb, and still can’t resist starting a new series at midnight.

For the rest of my story—career, how I work, and how to reach me—see my About me page (same source as the viveksinha/viveksinha site on GitHub).

This site started as a data experiment: export my IMDb history, visualize the patterns, and figure out what I actually like versus what I think I like. Turns out the answer is clear — thrillers, espionage, Nordic noir, and anything with moral ambiguity and subtitles.

What we publish

  • Curated lists built around mood, region, and era — not just “Top 10” rankings.
  • Under the Radar picks — titles we rated highly that most people haven’t found yet.
  • Upcoming — a timeline of releases we’re tracking, with filters when you want a narrower lens.
  • A full searchable archive of everything we’ve watched, with honest ratings.

The editorial lens leans into tension, slow burns, and stories that trust the audience. If it’s a Scandinavian crime drama or a Cold War spy thriller, it’s probably already on the list. If it’s a superhero franchise, it probably isn’t.

All recommendations are backed by data — roughly 8,500+ hours across 1,000+ titles, years of watching, and zero sponsored content.
